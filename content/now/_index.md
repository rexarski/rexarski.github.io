### Books

- Python
  - [Machine Learning with Python Cookbook](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/machine-learning-with/9781491989371/) by Chris Albon.
  - [Automate the Boring Stuf with Python](https://automatetheboringstuff.com/) by Al Sweigart.
  - [Python3网络爬虫开发实践](https://python3webspider.cuiqingcai.com/)，崔庆才著。
- R
  - [R in Action, 2nd ed](https://www.manning.com/books/r-in-action-second-edition) by Robert Kabacoff.
  - [R for Data Science](https://r4ds.had.co.nz/) by Hadley Wickham & Garret Grolemund.
  - [Advanced R](https://adv-r.hadley.nz/) by Hadley Wickham.
  - [Practical Data Science with R (2nd edition)](https://www.manning.com/books/practical-data-science-with-r-second-edition) by Nina Zumel and John Mount.
  - [Mastering Shiny](https://mastering-shiny.org/) by Hadley Wickham.
- JavaScript
  - [JavaScript for R](https://book.javascript-for-r.com/) by John Coene.
- Visualization
  - [Fullstack D3](https://www.newline.co/fullstack-d3) by Amelia Wattenberger.
  - [Interactive Data Visualization for the Web](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/interactive-data-visualization/9781491921296/) by Scott Murray.
  - All books written by Edward Tufte.
- NLP
  - [Text Mining with R](https://www.tidytextmining.com/) by Julia Silge & David Robinson.
- Stats
  - [Introduction to Modern Statistics](https://openintro-ims.netlify.app/) by Mine Çetinkaya-Rundel and Johanna Hardin.
  - [Forecasting: Principles and Practice](https://otexts.com/fpp2/) by Rob J Hyndman and George Athanasopoulos.
  - *More to come!*
- Predictive Modeling
  - [Applied Predictive Modeling](http://appliedpredictivemodeling.com/) by Max Kuhn and Kjell Johnson.
  - [Tidy Modeling with R](https://www.tmwr.org/) by Max Kuhn and Julia Silge.
  - [The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book](http://themlbook.com/) by Andriy Burkov.
- Other generic data science topics
  - [Data Science at the Command Line (2nd ed)](https://www.datascienceatthecommandline.com/) by Jeroen Janssens.
  - [Pro Git](https://git-scm.com/book/en/v2) by Scott Chacon and Ben Straub.
